Description

A hand of medium skin tone emerges from the top right of the scene, dropping six coins into another hand coming from the bottom right of the scene. The coins, from top to bottom, are red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and violet. The background is yellow, with a wide, white diagonal stripe extending from the top left to the bottom right of the card.

Key words

Upright: charity, generosity, benevolence

Reversed: debt, strings attached, power imbalance

  • How can I balance my desire to help others with the need to take care of myself?
  • How can I create reciprocity in my relationships?
