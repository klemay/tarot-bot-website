Description

Ten yellow-orange ombre wands emerge from the bottom of the scene in order from shortest on the left to tallest on the right. A purple figure with long hair stands on the eighth wand and rests their right hand on the ninth. With their left hand they reach toward bright yellow stars hanging in a pink, clouded sky.

Key words

Upright: responsibility, hard work, need for delegation

Reversed: burnout, exhaustion

Reflection questions

  • Which responsibilities or obligations in my life feel like a burden?
  • How can I practice self-care and prioritize my well-being while still fulfilling my obligations?
  • Am I taking on too much at once? How can I prioritize my tasks and delegate responsibilities to avoid burnout?
