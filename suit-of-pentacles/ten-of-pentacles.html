Description

Five friends of varying complexions and hair colors hold pentacles in their hands. One person stands on a ladder with one hand holding a pentacle toward their friend on the ground while the other hand touches a pentacle in the sky. Five pentacles hang in the starry blue sky among pink clouds.

Key words

Upright: stability, legacy, prestige, wealth

Reversed: financial instability, lack of resources, familial discord

Reflection questions

  • What kind of legacy do I want to leave behind for others to celebrate and honor?
  • In which areas of my life do I feel the greatest sense of security and stability? How can I build on that?
  • What values and principles guide my approach to family, community, and tradition?
