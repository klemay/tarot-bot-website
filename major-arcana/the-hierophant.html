The Hierophant Upright

How has your heritage shaped your identity? How has your identity affected the way you move through the world? What can you do to honor and celebrate that? The Hierophant represents the spiritual (not necessarily religious) teachings and beliefs that are passed down through generations. They remind us to take stock of those beliefs, keep what serves us, and let go of the rest. Form your own code of ethics and act accordingly while considering the example you are setting for others.
