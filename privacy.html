Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20 July 2023

The humans behind Tarot Bot are committed to protecting your privacy. There are four components to Tarot Bot covered by this Privacy Policy: (1) the Website (https://tarotbot.cards), (2) Tarot Bot’s social media profiles (on Bluesky, Cohost, and Tumblr), (3) the Bot that can be installed in Discord or Slack, and (4) the Tarot Bot API. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect and use your data when you visit the website, interact with our social media, use the Bot in Discord or Slack, or work with the Tarot Bot API.

By using the Website or interacting with our Social Media Profiles, Bot, or API, you signify your agreement with this Privacy Policy. Please read this policy carefully and check back periodically for updates.

1. Information collection and use

1.1 Personally-Identifiable Information

We do not automatically collect personally identifiable information about you. If you have personally-identifiable information in your social media profiles when interacting with Tarot Bot social media profiles, we will be able to see that, but we will not collect or store it.

1.2 Non Personally-Identifiable Information

For the Website, we use analytics providers (Google Analytics and Fullstory) to gather browser type, location, device information, and website usage data. This data is not tied to your IP address or any other personally-identifiable information. It is used in aggregate to improve the user experience of the website.

1.3 Tarot Bot Prompts

When interacting with the Discord Bot, Slack Bot, or API, you can optionally provide a prompt for your tarot reading. We do not collect or store these prompts, though we encourage you to check the Discord and Slack Privacy Policies.

1.4 API queries

Anonymized queries are collected for improving the functionality and user experience of the API.

2. Data Sharing and Disclosure

2.1 Your personal information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for marketing purposes.

2.2 Children’s Privacy

The Website, Social Media Profiles, API, and Bot are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

3. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The revised policy will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at hello@tarotbot.cards.
