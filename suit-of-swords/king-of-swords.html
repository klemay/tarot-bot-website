Description

A person with long hair, shrouded in light blue and wearing a cape, kneels at the center of a large blue wave. They seem to hold the water at bay by holding their sword straight up for protection.

Key words

Upright: discipline, truth, order, clarity

Reversed: manipulation, cruelty, malice, mental abuse

Reflection questions

  • How can I lead with clarity and conviction while also seeking to understand other perspectives?
  • How can I use my privilege to empower and support others?
  • How can I cultivate greater honesty and transparency in my communication and relationships?

King of Swords Upright

The King of Swords is intellectual, analytical, and a good communicator. They know how to get to the root cause of a situation immediately. They uphold rigid expectations for themselves and others, and they are impartial in their leadership and decision-making. They remind you to use logic and intellect to find your path forward.
